Welcome to Brew House!

Brew House is an online store and community for beer enthusiasts and breweries from all over the world. Consumers can search a large inventory of beers and breweries, place orders, and leave reviews for their favorite (or least favorite) brews. Brewers can view immediate feedback from their beer drinkers and respond directly to each user with a personalized response. Brew House shortens the physical and figurative distances between the beer enthusiast and brewery. Consumers now have countless new beers at their fingertips and breweries are reaching regions and markets that they never thought possible! Drink on!
